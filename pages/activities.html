ACTIVITIES

The Invisible Bottle

Fill a clear plastic bottle with water and put it in the pool. It's much trickier to find than a regular pool toy, as it camouflages into the water. Just make sure you keep a rough eye on where it ends up!

Pool Noodle Race

The game begins with the person in the middle saying his or her name, and then the name of another Scout (e.g “Alex calling Shannon!”). The person in the middle then races to the person whose name he or she called and tries to tag that person with the pool noodle.

Water Volleyball

Water volleyball (also called pool and acqua volleyball) is a competitive middle-court oriented water game. It can be played between two teams, each team consisting of 1 to 4 players, depending on the area of water in which the game is being played.

Balloon Popping

The objective is to pop every single balloon in the amount of moves by moving a balloon and creating a domino effect. If too many moves are made or not every balloon is popped after the set amount of moves, the game will end, prompting the player to retry or return to the main menu.

Dive for Treasure

Treasure Dive is a game about collecting loot to upgrade your character. Dive down and dodge fish while collecting trash, scrap, gems, and even a whole treasure chest which is worth so much money that you can actually cause an integer overflow (I'll fix it eventually).

Pool Scrabble

Write a letter on each sponge. Toss the sponges into a pool, or even the bathtub. Have each person draw 5 sponges to see if they can create a word. You can take turns and use Scrabble-like rules, or work as a team to create words together.
